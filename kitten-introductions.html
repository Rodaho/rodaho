Getting to know the breed

Kitten introductions

Tonkinese are often affectionate, lively, social cats. The right match begins with an honest picture of life together.

Companionship

Tonkinese commonly enjoy being involved in family life. Plan for daily interaction, play, and company rather than a hands-off pet.

Play and enrichment

Provide climbing space, sturdy scratching options, safe toys, and regular interactive play. Rotate toys to keep their curious minds engaged.

Settling in

Prepare a quiet room with food, water, litter, a bed, and hiding places. Introduce the wider home, children, and resident animals gradually.

Before bringing a kitten home

  • Arrange a veterinary registration and suitable pet insurance.
  • Kitten-proof wires, plants, windows, and small objects.
  • Choose a secure carrier and transport the kitten calmly.
  • Keep food and routine consistent at first; change things gradually.
  • Read the contract and ask any questions before collection day.
