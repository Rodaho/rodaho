Welcome to Rodaho

About us

Rodaho Tonkinese is a home-based cattery where kittens are raised as part of everyday family life.

Our home

Our kittens are raised indoors alongside family life, including children, dogs, and other cats. This helps them become accustomed to ordinary household sounds, gentle handling, and companionship from an early age.

Our commitment

Thoughtful homes

We take time to learn about prospective homes and aim to match each kitten carefully.

Clear information

We provide information about care, registration, health records, and the commitments involved before collection.

Ongoing support

We want every Rodaho kitten to thrive and ask new owners to contact us if their circumstances change.

Planning for a kitten

Our kittens are available for collection at 13 weeks, in line with GCCF recommendations. They are GCCF registered, supplied with a pedigree, flea treated and wormed if required, and fully vaccinated. Kittens are placed on the non-active register unless breeding has been agreed in advance.
