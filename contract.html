Responsible ownership

Rodaho kitten contract

The final written contract protects the kitten’s welfare and makes the breeder’s and new owner’s commitments clear.

Important

This is a plain-language overview, not a substitute for the signed Rodaho contract. The breeder should replace or supplement this page with the exact current contract, reviewed for the relevant law and circumstances.

What the agreement should cover

Kitten details

Identity, date of birth, parents, registration information, microchip details, and veterinary records.

Breeder commitments

What is provided at collection, health information disclosed, and ongoing guidance available to the new owner.

Owner commitments

Lifetime care, suitable food and veterinary attention, safe housing, and a commitment to contact the breeder if circumstances change.

Before signing

  • Read the complete agreement before paying a deposit or collecting a kitten.
  • Ask for clarification on any clause you do not understand.
  • Keep a signed copy with the kitten’s health and registration records.
  • Ensure any terms on neutering, breeding, insurance, and rehoming are stated clearly.
